Privacy Policy for Poultry360

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

Introduction

Poultry360 ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web dashboard for poultry farm management.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), full name, phone number
  • Profile Data: Profile pictures, organization name, role within organization
  • Contact Information: Support email, business contact details

Farm Management Data

  • Farm Records: Farm names, locations, batch information, flock details
  • Daily Records: Feed consumption, water usage, mortality records, weight measurements
  • Financial Data: Sales records, expense tracking, customer information, revenue data
  • Analytics: Performance metrics, production statistics, calculated insights

Device and Usage Information

  • Device Data: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for farm location tagging (only when you grant permission)
  • Camera and Photos: Access to camera and photo library for capturing and storing poultry records images
  • Usage Analytics: App features used, session duration, crash reports

Stored Locally

  • Offline Data: When offline mode is enabled, your farm data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • Authentication Tokens: Session tokens stored securely on your device for automatic login

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: Providing and maintaining farm management features
  • Account Management: Creating and managing your account, authentication
  • Data Synchronization: Syncing data between mobile app and web dashboard
  • Analytics and Insights: Generating performance reports and recommendations
  • Push Notifications: Sending reminders and alerts based on your notification settings
  • Customer Support: Responding to your requests and providing technical assistance
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraudulent or unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing algorithms
  • Secure Transmission: Data transmitted between app and servers uses HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control to ensure data privacy within organizations
  • Local Storage: Offline data stored securely on device with SQLite encryption

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Account data may be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations

We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Within Your Organization: Other users in your organization can access shared farm data based on their role
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate (hosting, analytics, push notifications)
    • Expo Push Notification Service (for notifications)
    • Cloud hosting providers (for data storage)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

Your Rights and Choices

Account Controls

  • Access Your Data: View and export all your data from the app
  • Update Information: Edit your profile and farm data at any time
  • Delete Account: Request account deletion from Profile Settings
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications and analytics in app settings

Location and Camera Permissions

  • Location Access: You can revoke location permissions in device settings; farm location features will be limited
  • Camera/Photos: You can revoke camera and photo library access; image upload features will be disabled

Offline Mode

  • Local Data: When using offline mode, data is stored locally on your device
  • Control: You can clear local data by logging out or uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

Poultry360 is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using Poultry360, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or integrate with third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Third-Party Services We Use:

  • Expo (React Native): App framework and push notifications
  • Cloud Hosting: For backend API and data storage
  • Analytics Services: For app performance monitoring

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of Poultry360 after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours via email and in-app notification, along with steps to protect your account.

  • Right to know what personal data is collected
  • Right to delete your personal data
  • Right to opt-out of data sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Your European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@poultry360.com
